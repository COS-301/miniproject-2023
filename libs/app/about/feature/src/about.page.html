About Us

Lapse is a social media app that puts the focus back on creating and sharing high-quality content. With a time limit to use the app, users are encouraged to make the most of their time and not get lost in endless scrolling. The time limit can increase based on engagements made on their posts, as well as a daily login reward.

But that's not all. Lapse also incentivizes high-quality content by allowing users to buy other posts for a certain price and earn a kickback on the engagement their purchase receives. This creates a community of creators and curators, where everyone benefits from great content.

So if you're tired of aimlessly scrolling and want to make the most of your time on social media, join Lapse and start creating and sharing content that truly stands out.
